Sandwich recipe

Description

A sandwich is a delicious combo of of a wheat-based exterior with a meat, veggie, and sauce-based interior

Ingredients

  • Panini press
  • Any wheat-based exterior (e.g., bread, wraps, etc.)
  • Preferred meat (e.g., turkey breast slices, ham, etc.)
  • Preferred veggies (e.g., lettuce, tomato, onion, hot pepper, avocado, pickles, etc.)
  • Preferred sauce (e.g., mustard, sriracha, mayo, etc.)

Steps

  1. Turn on panini press
  2. Put wheat product on a plate
  3. Add meat
  4. Add sliced veggies
  5. Add sauce
  6. Put sandwich in panini press for 3-4 mins
  7. Enjoy!
