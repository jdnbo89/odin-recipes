Bibimbap recipe

Description

Bibimbap is a delicious Korean dish that combines crispy rice with sauteed vegetables and ground beef, topped with a fried egg.

Bibimbap is both healthy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Zucchini
  • Red pepper
  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Eggs
  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Add 2 tbsps olive oil to a medium pot and heat over low-medium heat
  2. Mince garlic and dice onions into small pieces
  3. When the pot is hot, add the minced garlic, stirring to prevent burning
  4. When garlic is fragrant, add onions again stirring for 1-2 mins to prevent burning
  5. Add 2 cups rice to pot and stir for 1 min
  6. Add 4 cups water to the pot, bring the heat to medium-high until boiling, add 4 tbsps salt, then turn heat to low and let sit until water is fully absorbed
  7. Chop zucchini, carrots, and red pepper into long strips that are 1/4 inch wide
  8. Heat a frying pan with 2 tbsps sesmae oil over medium-high heat
  9. When the pan is hot, add red pepper
  10. Once red pepper is cooked, move to a plate and cover with tin foil
  11. Repeat for zucchini then carrots
  12. Mix together soy sauce and sesame oil and add ground beef to sauce
  13. Heat a medium pan over medium-high heat
  14. Add garlic and onions to the pan and stir for 1-2 mins to prevent burning
  15. Add meat to the pan and fry until no longer pink
  16. Divide rice amongst plates. Add vegetables in an organized fashion around the edges of the bowls
  17. In the same pan the meat was cooked in, crack an egg and fry for 2-3 mins
  18. Add the egg to the center of the vegetables
  19. Enjoy!
